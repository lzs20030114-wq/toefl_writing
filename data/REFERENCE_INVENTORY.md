## Tier meaning (most → least authentic)
- **official** — verbatim from an ETS-published PDF (Full-Length Practice Tests, Practice Sets). ETS-authored, but *practice material*, not leaked exam papers.
- **recalled** — reconstructed from an actually-administered 2026 exam (机经), dated & sourced. Real topic, third-party-reconstructed wording.
- **legacy** — in the repo before this audit, no recorded source; provenance unverifiable. Treat as practice-grade.
- **third_party** — collected from prep sites (goarno.io, toeflresources, magoosh, testsucceed…). Modeled on the task, NOT real exam items.
- **uncertain** — third-party, explicitly flagged as not traceable to any exam.
